Our Story

We Feed the
Night Owls

Knight Bite is a cloud kitchen built for the nocturnals — giving equal emphasis on food, technology, and logistics so that no one goes hungry after midnight.

How It Started

Born from a Simple Hunger

Knight Bite started with the idea to serve all the hungry nocturnals who find it really difficult to order late-night munchies. KB brought together late-night eaters and helped them order delicious meals at their convenience via the Knight Bite app and phone call.

Knight Bite is one such concept which was highly successful in adapting to the emerging market. Biters (our customers) accepted and loved the KB app that made the ordering process so much easier.

In just two weeks after the app's release, it was trending on top food apps and was downloaded by more than 5,000 users across Mangalore and Manipal.

Our Vision

Smiles Across Every City

Going forward, our aim is to help and bring smiles to many more faces and cover many more cities across India and around the world.

We believe great food at any hour is a right, not a privilege. Knight Bite is committed to making late-night delivery accessible, fast, and consistently delicious — wherever you are.
